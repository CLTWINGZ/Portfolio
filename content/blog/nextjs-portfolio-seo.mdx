## Why SEO matters for a software engineer portfolio

A portfolio is your search-friendly landing page. By combining strong metadata, structured content, and meaningful internal links, you can improve organic visibility for your name and specialty.

## Technical checklist for Next.js Portfolio SEO

- Use the Next.js metadata API on every route.
- Add JSON-LD for Person and WebSite schema.
- Generate sitemap and robots files automatically.
- Keep page titles unique and focused.

## Content checklist

Create blog posts around specific search intent such as **Next.js Portfolio SEO**, case studies, and performance benchmarks.
